7.0 inch LVDS Capacitive Touch Panel

August 20, 2016

Microtips Technology is excited to announce our 7.0 inch LVDS Capacitive touch panel, transmissive Normal White, with an operating temperature of -20 ~ 70 degrees celsius, and a storage temperature of -30 ~ 80 degrees celsius. our 7.0 inch LVDS display has an active area of 152.4x91.44 mm. The viewing direction is defined by the rubbing direction of its TFT surface treatments.

Furthermore, the grayscale inversion is located at the viewing directions as well. However, the optimal viewing directions for human viewing is normally located at the specified color which does not change to grayscale inversion. This would be the opposite side of the specified viewing direction in the specification above. There are 8 capacitive touch panel pins, with a system reset signal input pin. The input pin only has to be active low for this feature to work.

The lifetime on this display, is 50,000 hrs, or other-words the Half-Life-Time of these LED chips brightness which decreases to 50% than its original brightness, based on Ta 25+- 2 degrees Celsius and 60+-10% RH condition. This LCD display has 21 LEDs, with a typical 9.9v. The contrast ratio of 7.0 inch LVDS touch panel is 450:1 with a luminance of 450 candelas per square meter. We have also start developing flexible and transparent OLED panels, and will be feature in our next newsletter. Stay tune for more and exciting small display news.
