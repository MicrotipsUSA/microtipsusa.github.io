2016 Ultra Low Cost 4.3" TFT

March 20, 2017

Microtips is pleased to announce our new lowest cost 4.3" TFT module. This LCD display is available as a standard model with a 480x272px resolution (square pixels), 350nit (cd/m2) brightness, 500:1 contrast ratio, wide 140° viewing angle, standard -30 ~ 80°C temperature range, onboard graphics driver supporting both Sync & Data Enable interface modes, and a single voltage input (backlight driver not included). You can also choose to include an optional capacitive touch panel for capturing your application user’s input, with an onboard touch controller supporting I2C interface mode. The overall module size is 105.50 x 67.20 x 2.95mm, or 4.58mm thickness with touch panel.

We currently have a special limited time price of $13.99 for 5K per lot. Contact us for special pricing with other customizations or quantities to meet all your design needs!

Quick Features:

  • Diagonal Size: 4.3"
  • Resolution: 480 x 272px
  • Touch Panel: Resistive or Capacitive (Optional)
  • Brightness: 350nit (cd/m2)
  • Contrast: 500:1
  • Interface: 24bit, TTL - Sync/DE
  • Viewing Angles: 140° (6:00 optimal)
  • Operating Temp: -20 ~ 70°C
  • Storage Temp: -30 ~ 80°C
