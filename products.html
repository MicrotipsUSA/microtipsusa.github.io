Products

We are a leading custom touch panel and display manufacturer and offer a wide range of products and services. Our primary business revolves around LCD displays, PMOLED displays, and our PRECI-Touch™ capacitive touch panels. We are also a licensed processor of Corning® Gorilla® Glass for cover lens applications. We integrate our display, touch and cover lens products – as well as other value-added services – to build complete solutions for our customers. Our Standard Portfolio products include pre-designed display and PRECI-Touch™ panels and are great for low-volume orders or prototyping. If you’re searching for a trusted glass screen protector manufacturer, we have a strong history of glass production and offer a wide range of customization capabilities including packaging, screen printing, a variety of premium coatings, films and substrates, and much more.

Click on the links below to learn more about our products and capabilities.

TFT

A TFT LCD can give your product the perfect display. You can customize with different sizes, resolutions, and technologies like IPS.

OLED

An OLED LCD gives your product great resolution and rich, vibrant colors. Available in full-color Active Matrix or single-color Passive Matrix.

Touch Panel

A Touch Panel can bring your product and it's user closer together. We can provide a Capacitive or Resistive Touch Panel to ANY of our displays.

Character Monochrome

A Character Monochrome LCD is a simple, easy to read and use display solution. Although not as popular as before, we still provide fully custom character displays.

Graphic Monochrome

A Graphic Monochrome LCD can add a lot of customization to your product over a character display. We provide any size, resolution, and color.

Custom Segment

For when your project needs a truly iconic look. We can provide a Custom Segment LCD with any number of segments, custom icons, and in any color.
